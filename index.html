Rachel Ray's At Home Dog Biscuits

These might be the best dog biscuits your dog will ever snack on

By Rachel Hofer

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 3/4 cup nonfat powdered milk
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup hot water
