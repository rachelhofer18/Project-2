Rachel Ray's At Home Dog Biscuits

These might be the best dog biscuits your dog will ever snack on

By Rachel Hofer

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 3/4 cup nonfat powdered milk
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup hot water

Directions:

Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
You may have to use your hands as it will be very dry.
Pat it onto cookie sheet to about 1/2 in thick
Cut into shapes of your choice
You can let dry or bake in a 350 degree F oven
firm about 10-15 minutes
